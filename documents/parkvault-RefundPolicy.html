Cancellation & Refund Policy

Inventory Management & Reversal Logic

1. Perishable Inventory Notice

Parking slots are treated as time-sensitive, perishable inventory. Once a slot is reserved, it is removed from public availability, and the following refund schedule applies.

2. User-Initiated Cancellations

  • Standard Window: Cancellations made more than 2 hours prior to the Start Time are eligible for a 75% refund of the Base Price. Platform fees and GST are non-refundable.

  • Locked Window: Cancellations made within 2 hours of the Start Time, or after the Start Time has elapsed, are ineligible for a refund.

3. Platform-Initiated Cancellations

In the event that ParkVault or an on-site supervisor cancels a booking due to technical failure, slot unavailability, or operational errors not caused by the user, a 100% refund of the total transaction value will be issued.

4. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed automatically to the original source of payment (UPI, Credit/Debit Card, or Net Banking). Funds typically reflect in the user’s account within 5–7 business days.

5. Instant On-Site Bookings

Bookings facilitated manually by supervisors via the Admin Application are considered "Instant Bookings" and are strictly non-refundable once the OTP is validated.
