This Privacy Policy ("Policy") is an electronic record in the form of an electronic contract formed under the Information Technology Act, 2000. By accessing the ParkVault Platform, you consent to the collection, storage, and processing of your personal data as described herein.

3. Data Encryption and Security

We employ industry-standard SHA-256 security processes to encrypt sensitive information before it is stored in our secure database. We maintain strict physical and electronic safeguards to protect against unauthorized access or data breaches.
