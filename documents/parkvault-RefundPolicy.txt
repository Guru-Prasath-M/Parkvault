 Cancellation & Refund Policy :
 ---------------------------------
Inventory Management & Reversal Logic

1. PERISHABLE INVENTORY NOTICE Parking slots are treated as time-sensitive,
perishable inventory. Once a slot is reserved, it is removed from public availability, and the
following refund schedule applies:

2. USER-INITIATED CANCELLATIONS
 Standard Window: Cancellations made more than 2 hours prior to the Start Time
are eligible for a 75% refund of the Base Price. Platform fees and GST are nonrefundable.
 Locked Window: Cancellations made within 2 hours of the Start Time, or after the
Start Time has elapsed, are ineligible for a refund.

3. PLATFORM-INITIATED CANCELLATIONS In the event that ParkVault or an onsite supervisor cancels a booking due to technical failure, slot unavailability, or operational
errors not caused by the user, a 100% refund of the total transaction value will be issued.

4. REFUND PROCESSING Approved refunds are processed automatically to the original
source of payment (UPI, Credit/Debit Card, or Net Banking). Funds typically reflect in the
user’s account within 5–7 business days.

5. INSTANT ON-SITE BOOKINGS Bookings facilitated manually by supervisors via the
Admin Application are considered "Instant Bookings" and are strictly non-refundable once
the OTP is validated.
