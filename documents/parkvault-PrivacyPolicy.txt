your personal data as described herein.

2. DATA COLLECTION
 Personal Identifiers: We collect your name, email address, and mobile number.
Verification is conducted via a secure One-Time Password (OTP) system.
 Geolocation Data: To provide precise "Location Pins" and navigate you to assigned
parking slots, the App requires access to your device’s real-time GPS coordinates.

enable automated check-in and security verification.

3. DATA ENCRYPTION AND SECURITY We employ industry-standard SHA-256
security processes to encrypt sensitive information before it is stored in our secure database.

gateways for transaction processing and with authorized on-site supervisors for the sole
